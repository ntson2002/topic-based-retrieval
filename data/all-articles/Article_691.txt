Cancellation of Contracts for Life Annuities
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In cases where the obligor in a life annuity has received the principal for the life annuity , if the obligor fails to pay the life annuity or fails to perform other obligations , the other party may demand the return of the principal .
In such cases , the other party must return the amount of the life annuity he/she has already received to the obligor of the life annuity , less the amount of the interest on that principal .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of the preceding paragraph shall not preclude claims for damages .
