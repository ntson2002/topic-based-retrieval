Person Who Has Parental Authority
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A child who has not attained the age of majority shall be subject to the parental authority of his/her parents .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If a child is an adopted child , he/she shall be subject to the parental authority of his/her adoptive parents .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
Parental authority shall be exercised jointly by married parents ; provided that if either parent is incapable of exercising parental authority , the other parent shall do so .
