Negotiorum Gestio Management of Business
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A person who commences the management of a business for another person without being obligated to do so -LRB- hereinafter in this Chapter referred to as `` Manager '' -RRB- must manage that business -LRB- hereinafter referred to as `` Management of Business '' -RRB- in accordance with the nature of the business , using the method that best conforms to the interests of that another person -LRB- the principal -RRB- .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The Manager must engage in Management of Business in accordance with the intentions of the principal if the Manager knows , or is able to conjecture that intention .
