Changes in Order of Priority of Mortgages
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The order of priority of mortgages may be changed with the agreement of all mortgagees ; provided , however , that , if there are interested persons , the consent of the same must be obtained .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The changes in order under the preceding paragraph shall not take effect unless registered .
