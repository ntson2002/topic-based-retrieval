If a guardian has received the property of a ward or the right of a third party against the ward , the ward may rescind that reception .
In this case , the provisions of Article 20 shall apply mutatis mutandis .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provision of the preceding paragraph shall not preclude the application of the provisions of Articles 121 to 126 inclusive .
