Indivisible Nature of Right to Cancel
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If one party is constituted of two or more persons , the cancellation of the contract may be effected only by , or against , all of those persons .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the case set forth in the preceding paragraph , if the right to cancel is extinguished with respect to one of the persons who constitute a party , it shall also be extinguished with respect to the other persons .
