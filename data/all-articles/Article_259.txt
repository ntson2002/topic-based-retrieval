Performance of Obligations regarding Co-ownership
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If one of the co-owners holds a claim regarding co-ownership against other co-owners , upon partition , the portion of the property in co-ownership that vests in the obligors may be appropriated for the performance of the same .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If it is necessary to sell the portion of the property in co-ownership that vests in the obligors to obtain the performance under the preceding paragraph , the obligee may demand the sale of the same .
