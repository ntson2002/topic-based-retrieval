Rescission of Ruling of Commencement of Assistance
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
When the cause provided in the main clause of paragraph 1 of Article 15 ceases to exist , the family court must rescind the ruling of commencement of assistance at the request of the person in question , his/her spouse , any relative within the fourth degree of kinship , the guardian of a minor , the supervisor of the guardian of a minor , the assistant , the supervisor of the assistant , or a public prosecutor .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
At the request of the person prescribed in the preceding paragraph , the family court may rescind , in whole or in part , the ruling under paragraph 1 of the preceding Article .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
In cases the ruling under paragraph 1 of the preceding Article and the order under paragraph 1 of Article 876-9 are to be rescinded in their entirety , the family court must rescind the ruling of commencement of assistance .
