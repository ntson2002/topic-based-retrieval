If a person to be adopted has not attained 15 years of age , his/her legal representative may give his/her consent to the adoption of that person on behalf of that person .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
Where a person to be adopted is cared for by one of his/her parents and that parent does not have parental authority in relation to the person but cares for the person in accordance with Article 766 , a legal representative shall obtain the consent of that parent before giving the consent referred to in the preceding paragraph .
The same shall apply in the case where one of said person 's parents has parental authority in relation to the person which is suspended .
