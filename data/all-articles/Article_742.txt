Grounds on Which Marriage is Void
Marriage shall be void only in the following cases : -LRB- i -RRB- if one of the parties has no intention to marry due to mistaken identity or other cause ; or -LRB- ii -RRB- if the parties do not lodge notification of marriage ; provided however , that the effect of marriage shall not be prevented merely because notification was not given in the form prescribed in paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 739 .
