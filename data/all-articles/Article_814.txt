Judicial Dissolution of Adoptive Relation
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Either of the parties to an adoption may , in the following cases only , bring an action for dissolution of adoptive relation : -LRB- i -RRB- if he/she has been abandoned in bad faith by the other party ; -LRB- ii -RRB- if it is not clear whether the other party is dead or alive for not less than three years ; or -LRB- iii -RRB- if there is any other material ground making it difficult to continue the adoptive relation .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provision of paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 770 shall apply mutatis mutandis to the cases listed in item -LRB- i -RRB- and item -LRB- ii -RRB- of the preceding paragraph .
