Public Notice of Search for Heir
If , after the expiration of the period in paragraph -LRB- 1 -RRB- of the preceding Article , it is still not evident whether an heir exists , the family court shall , at the request of an administrator of inherited property or a public prosecutor , give public notice to the effect that if there is an heir , he/she should assert his/her right within a fixed period .
In this case , the period shall be not less than six months .
