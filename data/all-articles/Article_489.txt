Statutory Allocation
In cases where neither the person who tenders the performance nor the person who receives such performance does not designate the allocation of performance pursuant to the provision of the preceding Article , the allocation shall be effected as stipulated in each of the following items : -LRB- i -RRB- if the obligations include those which are due and those which are not due yet , the applicable performance shall be allocated to those which are due ; -LRB- ii -RRB- if all obligations are due , or none of the obligations are due , the applicable performance shall be allocated in the order of the obligations which shall result in more benefit to the obligor when performed ; -LRB- iii -RRB- if all obligations would have equal benefit to the obligor when performed , the applicable performance shall be allocated in the order of the obligations which have , or should have , the earliest due date ; and -LRB- iv -RRB- the performance of obligations which are equal in terms of the matters listed in the preceding two items shall be allocated in proportion to the amount of each obligation .
