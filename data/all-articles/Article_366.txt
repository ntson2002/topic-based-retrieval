Collection of Claims by Pledgees
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A pledgee may directly collect the claim that is the subject matter of the pledge .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If monies are the subject matter of a pledged claim , the pledgee may collect the same to the extent of the portion that corresponds to the amount of the pledgee 's own claim .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If the due date of the pledged claim under the preceding paragraph arrives prior to the due date of the claim of the pledgee , the pledgee may have the third party obligor deposit that amount to be paid to the pledgee .
In such cases , the pledge shall exist over the amount so deposited .
-LRB- 4 -RRB- 
If the subject matter of the pledged claim is not monies , the pledgee shall have the pledge over the Thing received as performance of the obligation .
