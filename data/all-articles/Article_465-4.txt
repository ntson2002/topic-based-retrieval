Grounds for Determination of Principal in Contract for Revolving Guarantee on Loans
The principal for the principal obligation under a contract for revolving guarantee on loans shall be determined if : -LRB- i -RRB- an obligee has filed a petition for compulsory execution or exercise of any security interest with respect to a claim the subject - matter of which is payment of money on any property of the relevant principal obligor or guarantor ; provided , however , that this provision shall apply only in cases where the proceedings for the compulsory execution or exercise of the security interest have been commenced ; -LRB- ii -RRB- the relevant principal obligor or guarantor has become subject to a ruling of the commencement of bankruptcy procedures ; or -LRB- iii -RRB- the relevant principal obligor or guarantor has died .
