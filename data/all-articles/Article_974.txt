Causes of Disqualification of Witness or Observer
The following persons may not be a witness or observer to a will : -LRB- i -RRB- a minor ; -LRB- ii -RRB- a presumed heir , donee , or a spouse or lineal relative of either ; or -LRB- iii -RRB- a spouse , relative within four degrees , secretary , or employee of a notary public .
