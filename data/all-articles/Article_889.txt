Right of Inheritance of Lineal Ascendant and Sibling
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In the case where there is no person to become an heir pursuant to the provisions of Article 887 , the following persons shall become heirs in accordance with the following order of rank : -LRB- i -RRB- lineal ascendants of the decedent ; provided that between persons of differing degree of kinship , the person who is of closer relationship shall have higher priority of inheritance ; -LRB- ii -RRB- siblings of the decedent .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 887 shall apply mutatis mutandis to the case referred to in item -LRB- ii -RRB- of the preceding paragraph .
