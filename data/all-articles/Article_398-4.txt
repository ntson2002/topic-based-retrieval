Alterations in Scope of Secured Claims under Revolving Mortgages and of Obligors
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The scope of the claims to be secured by a Revolving Mortgage may be altered if the alteration is effected before the principal is fixed .
The same shall likewise apply with respect to alterations of obligors .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In order to effect the alterations under the preceding paragraph , it is not required that the approval of third parties including subordinated obligees be obtained .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If the alteration under Paragraph 1 is not registered before the principal is fixed , it shall be deemed that such alteration was not effected .
