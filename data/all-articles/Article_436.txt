Setoffs by One Joint and Several Obligor
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In cases one joint and several obligor has a claim vis-a-vis the obligee , if such joint and several obligor invokes a setoff , the claim shall be extinguished for the benefit of all joint and several obligors .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
So long as the joint and several obligor who has the claim set forth in the preceding paragraph does not invoke the set-off , other joint and several obligor -LRB- s -RRB- may invoke the set-off solely to the extent of the portion of the obligation which is borne by such joint and several obligor .
