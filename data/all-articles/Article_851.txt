Duties of Supervisor of Guardian
The duties of a supervisor of a guardian are as follows : -LRB- i -RRB- to supervise the affairs of a guardian ; -LRB- ii -RRB- to apply to the family court without delay to appoint a guardian in the case where any vacancy in the position of a guardian occurs ; -LRB- iii -RRB- to take necessary measures in the case of an emergency ; and -LRB- iv -RRB- to represent the ward in conduct where there is a conflict of interest between the ward and the guardian or his/her representative .
