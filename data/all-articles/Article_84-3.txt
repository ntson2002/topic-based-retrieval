A director , auditor-secretary , or liquidator of a juridical person shall be made subject to a civil fine of not more than 500,000 Yen if he/she : -LRB- i -RRB- fails to effect any registration provided in this Chapter ; -LRB- ii -RRB- violates the provision of Article 51 , or makes any false entry in the inventory of property or directory of members ; -LRB- iii -RRB- has obstructed any inspection by the competent government agency , any government agency of the national government to which the authorities of the competent government agency are delegated , or any prefectural executive agency which administers the execution of the authorities of the competent government agency , or the court pursuant to the provision of paragraph 3 of Article 67 or paragraph 2 of Article 82 ; -LRB- iv -RRB- violates any order for supervisory purpose issued by the competent government agency , any government agency of the national government to which the authorities of the competent government agency are delegated , or any prefectural executive agency which administers the execution of the authorities of the competent government agency pursuant to the provision of paragraph 2 of Article 67 ; -LRB- v -RRB- has made any misrepresentation to , or has concealed any fact from , any government agency , any prefectural executive agency which administers the execution of the authorities of the competent government agency , or the general meeting ; -LRB- vi -RRB- fails to file a petition for the commencement of bankruptcy procedures pursuant to the provision of paragraph 2 of Article 70 or paragraph 1 of Article 81 ; or -LRB- vii -RRB- has failed to make the public notice required under paragraph 1 of Article 79 or paragraph 1 of Article 81 , or has made any improper public notice .
