Acceptance of Notification of Dissolution of Adoptive Relation
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
No notification of dissolution of adoptive relation shall be accepted until it has been found not to violate any of the provisions of paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 739 applied mutatis mutandis to the preceding Article , Article 811 and Article 811-2 , or the provisions of any other laws and regulations .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
Where the notification of dissolution of adoptive relation has been accepted in violation of the provisions of the preceding paragraph , the dissolution is not prevented from taking effect due to the violation .
