Effect of Fulfillment of Conditions
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A juristic act which is subject to a condition precedent shall become effective upon fulfillment of the condition .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
A juristic act which is subject to a condition subsequent shall become ineffective upon fulfillment of the condition .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If the party manifests an intention to extend the effect of fulfillment of the condition retroactively to any time prior to the time of the fulfillment , such intention shall prevail .
