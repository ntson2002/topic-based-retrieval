Person Who Has Parental Authority in the Case of Divorce or Recognition
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If parents divorce by agreement , they may agree upon which parent shall have parental authority in relation to a child .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the case of judicial divorce , the court shall determine which parent shall have parental authority .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
In the case where parents divorce before the birth of a child , the mother shall exercise parental rights and duties ; provided that the parties may agree that the father shall have parental authority after the child is born .
-LRB- 4 -RRB- 
A father shall only exercise parental authority with regard to a child of his that he has affiliated if both parents agree that he shall have parental authority .
-LRB- 5 -RRB- 
When the parents do not , or can not , make the agreements referred to in paragraph -LRB- 1 -RRB- , paragraph -LRB- 3 -RRB- , and the preceding paragraph , the family court may , at the request of the father or the mother , make a ruling in lieu of agreement .
-LRB- 6 -RRB- 
The family court may , at the request of any relative of the child , rule that the other parent shall have parental authority in relation to the child if it finds it necessary for the interests of the child .
