Causes of Dissolution of Juridical Person
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A juridical person shall be dissolved because of : -LRB- i -RRB- the occurrence of any cause of dissolution provided in the articles of incorporation or act of endowment , as the case may be ; -LRB- ii -RRB- the successful consummation of the business which is the purpose of the juridical person , or the impossibility of such successful consummation ; -LRB- iii -RRB- the ruling to commence bankruptcy procedures ; or -LRB- iv -RRB- the rescission of the permission of the establishment .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In addition to the causes listed in the respective items of the preceding paragraph , an incorporated association shall be dissolved because of : -LRB- i -RRB- the applicable resolution of the general meeting ; or -LRB- ii -RRB- the attrition of all members .
