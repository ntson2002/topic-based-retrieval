Seller's Warranty where Rights Partially Belonged to Others
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If the seller can not transfer any part of the rights which are the subject matter of the sale because the part of the rights belongs to others , the seller may demand a reduction of the purchase money in proportion to the value of the part in shortage .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the cases set forth in the preceding paragraph , a buyer in good faith may cancel the contract if the buyer would not have bought the rights if the rights consisted only of the remaining portion .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
A demand for the reduction in the purchase money or cancellation of the contract shall not preclude a buyer in good faith from making a claim for damages .
