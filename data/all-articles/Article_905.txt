Recovery Right of Share of Inheritance
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If one joint heir assigns his/her share of inheritance to a third party before a division of the inherited property , any other joint heir may obtain the share through the reimbursement of the value and expenses of that and recover the share in inheritance .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The right of the preceding paragraph shall be exercised within one month .
