Acceptance of Notification of Divorce
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Notification of divorce may not be accepted unless the divorce has been found not to violate the provision of paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 739 applied mutatis mutandis to the preceding Article , paragraph -LRB- 1 -RRB- of Article 819 , or the provisions of any other laws and regulations .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If notification of divorce has been accepted despite the violation of the provisions of the preceding paragraph , the effect of the divorce shall not be prevented because of this violation .
