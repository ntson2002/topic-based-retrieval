Effect of Rescission of Marriage
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Rescission of marriage shall not have retroactive effect .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
A party who did not know of the cause of rescission at the time of marriage must return property obtained by the marriage to the extent of the gain he/she actually receives .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
A party who was aware of the cause of rescission at the time of marriage must return all of the gain obtained by the marriage .
In this case , the party is liable to compensate an adverse party without knowledge for damages .
