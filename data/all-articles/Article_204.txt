Grounds for Extinction of Agent's Possessory Rights
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In cases where a person possesses a Thing through an agent , possessory rights shall be extinguished on the grounds listed below : -LRB- i -RRB- That the principal renounces his/her intention to have his/her agent possess ; -LRB- ii -RRB- That the agent manifests his/her intention to the principal to thenceforward possess the possessed Thing on behalf of himself/herself or a third party ; or -LRB- iii -RRB- That the agent has lost the direct control over the possessed Thing .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
Possessory rights shall not be extinguished solely as a result of the extinction of the power of representation .
