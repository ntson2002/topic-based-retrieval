Performance from Heir's Own Property
A person who has made an application for separation of property or a person who has applied for entry into distribution proceedings may exercise his/her rights against an heir 's own property only in the case where he/she was not able to receive performance in full from the inherited property .
In this case , this person may receive performance with priority over the obligees of an heir .
