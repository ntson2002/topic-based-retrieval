Statutory Liens for Expenses for Common Benefit
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Statutory liens for expenses for the common benefit shall exist with respect to the expenses of preservation , liquidation or distribution of the property of the obligor incurred for the common benefit of all obligee .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
With respect to expenses that were not beneficial for all obligees , a statutory lien shall exist solely for obligees who received a benefit as a result of such expenses .
