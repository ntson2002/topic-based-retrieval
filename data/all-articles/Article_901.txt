Statutory Share in Inheritance of Heirs per Stirpes
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The share in inheritance of a lineal descendant who becomes an heir pursuant to the provisions of paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- or paragraph -LRB- 3 -RRB- of Article 887 shall be the same as the share that person 's lineal ascendant would have received ; provided that if there are two or more lineal descendants , their shares in inheritance shall be determined in accordance with the provisions of the preceding Article .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provision of the preceding paragraph shall apply mutatis mutandis to the case where a child of a sibling becomes an heir pursuant to the provision of paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 889 .
