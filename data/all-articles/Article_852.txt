Application Mutatis Mutandis of Mandate and Guardian Provisions
The provisions of Article 644 , Article 654 , Article 655 , Article 844 , Article 846 , Article 847 , paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 861 , and Article 862 shall apply mutatis mutandis to a supervisor of a guardian , the provisions of paragraph -LRB- 3 -RRB- of Article 840 and Article 857-2 shall apply mutatis mutandis to a supervisor of a guardian of a minor , and the provisions of paragraph -LRB- 4 -RRB- of Article 843 , Article 859-2 and Article 859-3 shall apply mutatis mutandis to a supervisor of a guardian of an adult .
