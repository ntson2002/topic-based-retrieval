Rescission of Ruling of Loss of Parental Authority, Suspension of Parental Authority or Loss of Right of Administration of Property
If the causes in the main clause of Article 834 , paragraph -LRB- 1 -RRB- of Article 834-2 , or the preceding Article have ceased to exist , the family court may , at the request of the person concerned or any relative of his/hers , rescind a ruling of loss of parental authority , suspension of parental authority , or loss of the right of administration of property .
