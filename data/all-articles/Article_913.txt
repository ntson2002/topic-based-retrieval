Share of Liability to Guarantee Insolvent Joint Heir
If there is an insolvent joint heir who is liable to guarantee other joint heirs , the portion of the liability which the insolvent joint heir is to bear shall be apportioned amongst other joint heirs with a right to reimbursement , and other solvent joint heirs shall contribute to the portion unable to be reimbursed according to the share in inheritance of each ; provided that if there is negligence on the part of the person who seeks reimbursement , he/she may not make a claim against other another joint heir to contribute .
