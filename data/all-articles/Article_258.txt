Partition of property in co-ownership by Judgment
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If no agreement is reached among co-owners with respect to the partition of property in co-ownership , a demand for partition of the same may be submitted to the court .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In cases provided for in the preceding paragraph , if the property in co-ownership can not be partitioned in kind , or it is likely that the value thereof will be significantly reduced by the partition , the court may order the sale of the same at auction .
