Disinheritance of Presumed Heir by Will
If a decedent has indicated an intention by will to disinherit a presumed heir , the executor of that will shall apply to the family court for disinheritance of the presumed heir without delay after the will has taken effect .
In this case , the disinheritance of that presumed heir shall have retroactive effect from the time of the decedent 's death .
