Affiliation of Unborn Child or Child who has Died
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A father may also affiliate his unborn child .
In this case , the mother 's consent shall be obtained .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If a child has died , a father or mother may still give affiliation , limited to the case where that child had a lineal descendant .
In this case , if that lineal descendant is an adult , his/her consent shall be obtained .
