Content of Rights of Retention
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If a possessor of a Thing belonging to another person has a claim that has arisen with respect to that Thing , he/she may retain that thing until that claim is satisfied ; provided , however , that this shall not apply if such claim has not yet fallen due .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of the preceding paragraph shall not apply in cases where possession commenced by means of a tortious act .
