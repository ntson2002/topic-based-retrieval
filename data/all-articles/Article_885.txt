Expenses relating to Inherited Property
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Expenses relating to inherited property shall be paid out of that property ; provided that this shall not apply to expenses resulting from the negligence of an heir .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The expenses of the preceding paragraph are not required to be paid out of property obtained by a claimant to statutory reserved portion through abatement of a gift .
