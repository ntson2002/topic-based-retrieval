Exercise of Parental Authority on Behalf of Minor Ward
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A guardian of a minor shall exercise parental authority in lieu of a minor ward with respect to that minor ward 's child .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of Articles 853 to 857 inclusive and Articles 861 to 866 inclusive shall apply mutatis mutandis to the case referred to in the preceding paragraph .
