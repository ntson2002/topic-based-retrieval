Duties and Authority of Liquidator
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A liquidator shall have the duties to : -LRB- i -RRB- conclude the current business ; -LRB- ii -RRB- collect debts and perform obligations ; and -LRB- iii -RRB- deliver the residual assets .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The liquidator may perform any and all acts in order to perform its duties listed in the respective items of the preceding paragraph .
