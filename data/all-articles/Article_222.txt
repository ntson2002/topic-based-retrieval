Construction and Use of Dams
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If the owner of land containing a stream needs to construct a dam , he/she may construct that dam by fixing it to the other side even if the land on the other side is owned by others ; provided , however , that he/she must pay compensation for damages arising as a result .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The owner of the land on the other side may use the dam under the preceding paragraph if he/she owns part of the land containing the stream .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
The provisions of Paragraph 2 of the preceding article shall apply mutatis mutandis to the cases provided for in the preceding paragraph .
