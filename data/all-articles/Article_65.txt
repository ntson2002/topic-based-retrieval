Voting Right of Members
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The vote of each member shall be of equal value .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
A member who is not present in the general meeting may vote in writing or by proxy .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
The provisions of the preceding two paragraphs shall not apply if the articles of incorporation provide otherwise .
