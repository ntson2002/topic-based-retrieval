Participation in Partition of property in co-ownership
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Persons who hold rights with respect to property in co-ownership and the obligee of any co-owner may participate in partitions at their own expense .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If , notwithstanding a request for participation under the provisions of the preceding paragraph , partition is effected without allowing the participation of the person who submitted the request , that partition may not be asserted against the person who submitted the request .
