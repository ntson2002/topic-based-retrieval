Effect of Cancellation
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If one of the parties exercises his/her right to cancel , each party shall assume an obligation to restore the other party to that other party 's original position ; provided , however , that this shall not prejudice the rights of a third party .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the case set forth in the main clause of the preceding paragraph , if any monies are to be refunded , interest must accrue from the time of the receipt of those monies .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
The exercise of the right to cancel shall not preclude claims for damages .
