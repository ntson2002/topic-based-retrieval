Use of Deposited Thing and Retention by Third Parties
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A depositary may not use , or allow third parties to retain , the Thing deposited without obtaining the consent of the depositor .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of Article 105 and paragraph 2 of Article 107 shall apply mutatis mutandis to cases where a depositary may allow third parties to retain deposited Things .
