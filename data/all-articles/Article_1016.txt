Executor's Right to Appoint Subagent
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
An executor may not allow a third party to undertake the duties of an executor unless there are justifiable reasons ; provided , however , that this shall not apply if the testator has indicated a contrary intent in his/her will .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the case where an executor has allowed a third party to undertake the duties of an executor pursuant to the provision in the proviso to the preceding paragraph , the executor shall owe the responsibility referred to in Article 105 to the heir -LRB- s -RRB- .
