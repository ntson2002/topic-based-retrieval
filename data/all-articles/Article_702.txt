Managers' Claims for Reimbursement of Costs
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If a Manager has incurred useful expenses for a principal , the Manager may claim reimbursement of those costs from the principal .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of Paragraph 2 of Article 650 shall apply mutatis mutandis to cases where a Manager has incurred useful obligations on behalf of the principal .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If a Manager has engaged in the Management of Business against the intention of the principal , the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs shall apply mutatis mutandis , solely to the extent the principal is actually enriched .
