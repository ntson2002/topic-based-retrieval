Provision of Security by and Remuneration for Administrator
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The family court may require an administrator to provide reasonable security with respect to the administration and return of the property .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The family court may grant reasonable remuneration to the administrator from the property of the absentee with due regard to the relationship between the administrator and absentee and other circumstances .
