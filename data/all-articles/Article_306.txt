General Statutory Lien
A person who has a claim that arose from the causes listed below shall have a statutory lien over the entire property of the obligor : -LRB- i -RRB- Expenses for the common benefit ; -LRB- ii -RRB- An employer-employee relationship ; -LRB- iii -RRB- Funeral expenses ; or -LRB- iv -RRB- The supply of daily necessaries .
