Matters to be Registered upon Registration of Formation and Registration of Change
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The following matters shall be registered upon registration of establishment of any juridical person : -LRB- i -RRB- Purpose -LRB- s -RRB- ; -LRB- ii -RRB- Name ; -LRB- iii -RRB- Location of the office ; -LRB- iv -RRB- Date of the permission of the establishment ; -LRB- v -RRB- Term of existence , if such term is stipulated ; -LRB- vi -RRB- Total amount of assets ; -LRB- vii -RRB- Method of contribution , if such method is defined ; and -LRB- viii -RRB- Name and domicile of each director .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If there is any change in any matter listed in the respective items of the preceding paragraph , the registration of the change must be filed within two weeks at the location of its principal office , and within three weeks at any location of its other office .
In each of the above cases , the change may not be asserted against a third party before its registration .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If there is any ruling for the provisional disposition which suspends the execution of the duties of any director , or appoints any person who executes such duties in place of a director , or if there is any ruling to change or rescind such provisional disposition , the registration of such fact must be made at the location of the principal office or other office .
The provision of the second sentence of the preceding paragraph shall apply mutatis mutandis to such case .
