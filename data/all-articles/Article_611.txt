Demands for Reduction of Rent due to Partial Loss of Leased Thing
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If any part of a leased thing is lost due to reasons not attributable to the negligence of the lessee , the lessee may demand a reduction of the rent in proportion to the value of the lost part .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the cases set forth in the preceding paragraph , if the lessee can not achieve the purpose of the lease with the remaining portion only , the lessee may cancel the contract .
