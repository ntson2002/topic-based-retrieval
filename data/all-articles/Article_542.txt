Right to Cancel for Delayed Performance where Time is of the Essence
In cases where , due to the nature of the contract or a manifestation of intention by the parties , the purpose of the contract can not be achieved unless the performance is carried out at a specific time and date or within a certain period of time , if one of the parties has failed to perform at the time that period lapses , the other party may immediately cancel the contract without making the demand referred to in the preceding Article .
