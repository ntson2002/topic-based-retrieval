Permission for Minors to Carry on Business
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A minor who is permitted to carry on one or more kinds of business shall have the same capacity to act as a person of the age of majority as far as such business is concerned .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the case set forth in the preceding paragraph , if the minor may be unable to perform the relevant business for any reason , his/her statutory agent may revoke or limit permission in accordance with the provisions of Part IV -LRB- Relatives -RRB- .
