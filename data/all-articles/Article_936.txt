Administration of Inherited Property where Two or more Heirs
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In the case where there are two or more heirs , the family court shall appoint an administrator of the inherited property from amongst the heirs .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The administrator of the inherited property of the preceding paragraph shall undertake all necessary acts to administer the inherited property and perform any obligation on behalf of the heirs .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
The provisions of Article 926 to 935 inclusive shall apply mutatis mutandis to an administrator of the inherited property of paragraph -LRB- 1 -RRB- .
In this case , ` within five days of making that qualified acceptance ' in paragraph -LRB- 1 -RRB- of Article 927 shall be read as ` within ten days of the appointment of an administrator of the inherited property ' .
