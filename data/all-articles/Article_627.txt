Offer to Terminate Employment with Indefinite Term
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If the parties have not specified the term of employment , either party may request to terminate at any time .
In such cases , employment shall terminate on the expiration of two weeks from the day of the request to terminate .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If remuneration is specified with reference to a period , the request to terminate may be made with respect to the following period of time onward ; provided , however , that the request to terminate must be made in the first half of the current period .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If remuneration is specified with reference to a period of six months or more , the request to terminate under the preceding paragraph must be made three months before the termination .
