Demands for Partition of property in co-ownership
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Each co-owner may demand the partition of property in co-ownership at any time ; provided , however , that this shall not preclude concluding a contract to the effect that a partition will not occur for a period within five years .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The contract under the proviso to the preceding paragraph may be renewed ; provided , however , that the period thereof may not exceed five years from the time of the renewal .
