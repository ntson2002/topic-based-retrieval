Actions for Recovery of Possession
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
When a possessor is forcibly dispossessed , he/she may claim for the restoration of the Thing and compensation for damages by bringing an action for recovery of possession .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
An Action for recovery of possession can not be filed against a specific successor of the usurper of possession ; provided , however , that this shall not apply if that successor had knowledge of the fact of usurpation .
