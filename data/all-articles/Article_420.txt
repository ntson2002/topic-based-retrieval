Liquidated Damages
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The parties may agree on the amount of the liquidated damages with respect to the failure to perform the obligation .
In such case , the court may not increase or decrease the amount thereof .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The liquidated damages shall not preclude the demand for performance or the exercise of the cancellation right .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
Any penalty is presumed to constitute liquidated damages .
