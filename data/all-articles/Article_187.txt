Succession to Possession
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A successor to a possessor may , at the option of the successor , assert either his/her possession only , or his/her possession together with that of the predecessor .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In cases where a person asserts the possession of the predecessor together with his/her own , he/she shall also succeed to defects in the same .
