Compensation by Value to Claimant for Statutory Reserved Portion
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A beneficiary or donee may avoid a duty to refund by compensating a claimant to statutory reserved portion the value of the object of the gift or testamentary gift , to the extent subject to abatement .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provision of the preceding paragraph shall apply mutatis mutandis to the case referred to in the proviso to paragraph -LRB- 1 -RRB- of the preceding Article .
