Subrogation by Partial Performance
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If any performance by subrogation occurs with respect to any portion of a claim , the subrogee shall exercise his/her right together with the obligee in proportion to the value of his/her performance .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the case set forth in the preceding paragraph , the cancellation of a contract based on the failure to perform the obligation may be effected only by the obligee .
In such case , the obligee must reimburse to the subrogee the value of the performance he/she effected plus interest .
