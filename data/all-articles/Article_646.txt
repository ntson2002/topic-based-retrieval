Delivery of Received Things by Mandatary
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The mandatary must deliver to the mandator monies and other Things that he/she has received during the course of administering the mandated business .
The same shall apply to fruits the mandatary has reaped .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The mandatary must transfer to the mandator rights the mandatary has acquired in his/her own name on behalf of the mandator .
