Keeping the Thing Retained by Holders of Rights of Retention
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A holder of a right of retention must possess the Thing retained with the care of a good manager .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
A holder of rights of retention may not use , lease or give as a security the Thing retained unless he/she obtains the consent of the obligor ; provided , however , that this shall not apply to uses necessary for the preservation of that Thing .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If the holder of a right of retention violates the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs , the obligor may demand that the right of retention be extinguished .
