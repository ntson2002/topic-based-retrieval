Mandatary's Responsibility for Consumption of Monies
If the mandatary has consumed monies for his/her personal benefit that the mandatary is to deliver to the mandator , or any monies that are to be used for the benefit of the mandator , the mandatary must pay interest for the period from the day of that consumption .
In such cases , if any damages still remain , the mandatary shall be liable to compensate for the same .
