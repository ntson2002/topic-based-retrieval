Requests for Fixing of Principal of Revolving Mortgages
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If three years have elapsed from the time of the creation of a Revolving Mortgage , the revolving mortgagor may request the fixing of the principal secured .
In such cases , the principal secured shall be fixed when two weeks have elapsed since the time of that request .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
A revolving mortgagee may request the fixing of the principal secured at any time .
In such cases , the principal secured shall be fixed on the request of the same .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
The provisions of the preceding two paragraphs shall not apply in cases where the date on which the principal secured is to be fixed is prescribed .
