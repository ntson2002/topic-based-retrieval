Effect of Arrival of Assigned Time
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If time of commencement of validity is assigned to a juristic act , the performance of such juristic act may not be demanded before the arrival of such time .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If time of expiration of validity is assigned to a juristic act , the validity of such juristic act shall expire upon the arrival of such time .
