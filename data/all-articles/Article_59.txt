Duties of Auditor-Secretary
The duties of an auditor-secretary shall be : -LRB- i -RRB- to audit the status of the property of the juridical person ; -LRB- ii -RRB- to audit the status of the execution of the business by the director -LRB- s -RRB- ; -LRB- iii -RRB- to submit a report to the general meeting of the members or to the competent government agency when he/she finds any violation of the applicable laws and regulations , articles of incorporation or act of endowment , or any significant impropriety with respect to the status of the property or the execution of the business ; and -LRB- iv -RRB- to convoke a general meeting of the members when it is necessary to submit the report set forth in the preceding item .
