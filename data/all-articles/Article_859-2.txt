Exercise of Authority where Multiple Guardians of Adult
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If there are multiple guardians of an adult , the family court may determine ex officio that the guardians should exercise authority jointly or according to a division of labor .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The family court may rescind ex officio a determination made pursuant to the provisions of the preceding paragraph .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If there are multiple guardians of an adult , it is sufficient that a manifestation of intention by a third party be made to one guardian .
