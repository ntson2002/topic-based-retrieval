Ownership of Property between Husband and Wife
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Property owned by one party before marriage and property obtained in the name of that party during marriage shall be separate property -LRB- property owned singularly by one party to a marriage -RRB- .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
Property that does not clearly belong to either husband or wife shall be presumed to be held in co-ownership .
