Performance to Inheritance Obligees and Donees
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If the existence of an heir has not become evident within two months of the public notice of paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 952 , an administrator of inherited property shall , without delay , give public notice to all inheritance obligees and donees to the effect that a claim for performance should be made within a specified period .
In this case , the period shall be not less than two months .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of paragraphs -LRB- 2 -RRB- to -LRB- 4 -RRB- inclusive of Article 927 and Articles 928 to 935 -LRB- excluding the proviso to Article 932 -RRB- shall apply mutatis mutandis to the case referred to in the preceding paragraph .
