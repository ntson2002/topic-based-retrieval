Grounds for Termination of Mandate
A mandate shall terminate when : -LRB- i -RRB- The mandator or mandatary dies ; -LRB- ii -RRB- The mandator or mandatary is subject to a ruling for the commencement of bankruptcy procedures ; -LRB- iii -RRB- The mandatary is subject to an order for the commencement of guardianship .
