Dismissal and Resignation of Executor
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If an executor has failed to perform his/her duties , or if there is any other justifiable reason , an interested party may apply to the family court for the dismissal of that executor .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
An executor may resign from his or her duties with the permission of the family court if there is a justifiable reason .
