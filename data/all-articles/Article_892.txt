Disinheritance of Presumed Heir
A decedent may make an application to the family court for the disinheritance of a presumed heir -LRB- here and below , referring to a person who would otherwise become an heir upon the commencement of inheritance -RRB- who has a legally reserved portion if that person has abused or given grave insult to the decedent , or if there has been any other grave misconduct on the part of the presumed heir .
