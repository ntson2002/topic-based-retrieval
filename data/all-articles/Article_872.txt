A person who , as an ex-minor ward , made a contract with a guardian , or the heir of the guardian , after attaining majority but before settlement of the account of guardianship may rescind such contract .
The same shall apply to unilateral juristic acts that person makes toward a guardian of a minor or his/her successor .

The provisions of Article 20 and Articles 121 to 126 inclusive shall apply mutatis mutandis to the case referred to in the preceding paragraph .
