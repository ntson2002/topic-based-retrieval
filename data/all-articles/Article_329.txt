Order of Priority of General Statutory Liens
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In cases where there is conflict among general statutory liens , the order of priority shall follow the order listed in each item of Article 306 .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In cases where there is conflict between a general statutory lien and a special statutory lien , the special statutory lien shall prevail over the general statutory lien ; provided , however , that statutory liens on expenses for the common benefit shall have the effect of prevailing over all obligees who received the benefit of the same .
