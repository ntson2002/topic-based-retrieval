Novation
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If the parties conclude a contract which changes any element of an obligation , such obligation shall be extinguished by novation .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
It is deemed that an element of obligation has been changed if a conditional obligation is made an unconditional obligation , if a condition is added to an unconditional obligation , or if any condition on an obligation is changed .
