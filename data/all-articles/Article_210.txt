Right of Passage over Other Land for Access to Public Roads
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
An owner of land that is surrounded by other land and has no access to public roads may pass through the other land that surrounds his/her land to reach the public roads .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The preceding paragraph shall likewise apply in cases where an owner can not access the public roads unless he/she passes through ponds , lakes , rivers , waterways or seas , or in cases where there is a considerable difference in height between the land and the public road on account of a cliff .
