Probate of Will
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A custodian of a will , after coming to know of the commencement of inheritance , shall without delay submit the will to the family court and apply for probate .
In the case where there is no custodian of a will , the same shall apply after an heir discovers the will .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provision of the preceding paragraph shall not apply to a will made by notarized document .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
A will that has been sealed may not be opened unless in the family court in the attendance of an heir or his/her representative .
