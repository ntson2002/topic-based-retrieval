Commencement of Bankruptcy Procedures with respect to Juridical Person
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In cases a juridical person is unable to pay its debts in full out of its property , the court shall , at the filing of any director or any obligee or by exercising its authority , provide the ruling to commence bankruptcy procedures .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the case prescribed in the preceding paragraph , the director -LRB- s -RRB- must immediately file a petition for the commencement of bankruptcy procedure .
