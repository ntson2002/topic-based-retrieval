Effect of Performance in cases Any Property Delivered to Perform Obligation is Consumed or Assigned
In the case referred to in the preceding two Articles , if an obligee without knowledge consumes or assigns any property which he/she received as the performance of the obligation , such performance shall be effective .
In such case , if the relevant obligee has received any request for compensation from a third party , he/she shall not be precluded from seeking reimbursement from the person who performed the obligation .
