Allocation in cases Principal, Interest, and Expenses Should be Paid
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In cases where the obligor should pay the principal as well as the applicable interest and expenses with respect to one or more obligation -LRB- s -RRB- , if the person who must perform tenders any performance which is not sufficient to extinguish such obligation in its entirety , such performance must be allocated first to the expense , and then to the interest and principal , in this order .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provision of Article 489 shall apply mutatis mutandis to the cases set forth in the preceding paragraph .
