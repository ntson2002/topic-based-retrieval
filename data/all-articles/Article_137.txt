Forfeiture of Benefit of Time
The obligor may not assert the benefit of time if : -LRB- i -RRB- the obligor has become subject to the ruling of the commencement of bankruptcy procedures ; -LRB- ii -RRB- the obligor has destroyed , damaged , or diminished the security ; or -LRB- iii -RRB- the obligor fails to provide security when it has the obligation to do so .
