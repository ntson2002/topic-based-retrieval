Appurtenant Nature of Servitudes
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Servitudes are appurtenant to ownership in the dominant land -LRB- hereinafter referring to the land of a person entitled to a servitude , enjoying benefits from the land of others -RRB- and shall be transferred together with that ownership , or shall be the subject of other rights that exist in relation to the dominant land ; provided , however , that this shall not apply if the act establishing the servitude provides otherwise .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
Servitudes may neither be assigned nor made the subject of other rights apart from the dominant land .
