Duration of Contractor's Warranty
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The demand for repair or claim for damages and cancellation of the contract under the preceding three Articles must be made within one year from the time of the delivery of the subject matter of the work .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
Where no delivery of the subject matter is required , the period referred to in the preceding paragraph commences to run from the time of the completion of the work .
