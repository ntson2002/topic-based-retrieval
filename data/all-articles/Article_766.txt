If parents divorce by agreement , the matters of who will have custody over a child , visitation and other contacts between the father or mother and the child , sharing of expenses required for custody of the child and any other necessary matters regarding custody over the child shall be determined by that agreement .
In this case , the child 's interests shall be considered with the highest priority .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If the agreement set forth in the preceding paragraph has not been made , or can not be made , the matters set forth in the preceding paragraph shall be determined by the family court .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
The family court may change the agreement or determination under the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs and order any other proper disposition regarding custody over the child , if it finds this necessary .
-LRB- 4 -RRB- 
The rights and duties of parents beyond the scope of custody may not be altered by the provisions of the preceding three paragraphs .
