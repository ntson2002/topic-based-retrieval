Judicial Divorce
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Only in the cases stated in the following items may either husband or wife file a suit for divorce : -LRB- i -RRB- if a spouse has committed an act of unchastity ; -LRB- ii -RRB- if abandoned by a spouse in bad faith ; -LRB- iii -RRB- if it is not clear whether a spouse is dead or alive for not less than three years ; -LRB- iv -RRB- if a spouse is suffering from severe mental illness and there is no prospect of recovery ; or -LRB- v -RRB- if there is any other grave cause making it difficult to continue the marriage .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
A court may dismiss a suit for divorce if it finds continuing the marriage reasonable taking into account all circumstances , even in the case where there is a cause listed in items -LRB- i -RRB- to -LRB- iv -RRB- inclusive of the preceding paragraph .
