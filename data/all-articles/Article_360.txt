Duration of Pledges of Immovable Properties
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The duration of pledges of immovable properties can not exceed ten years .
Even if a longer period is provided for in the act establishing the pledge , the duration of the same shall be ten years .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The creation of pledges may be renewed ; provided , however , that the duration of the same can not exceed ten years from the time of the renewal .
