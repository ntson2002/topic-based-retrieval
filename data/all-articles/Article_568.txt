Warranty in cases of Compulsory Auctions
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The successful bidder at compulsory auction may cancel the contract or demand a reduction from the purchase money against the obligor in accordance with the provisions from Article 561 through to the preceding Article .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the cases set forth in the preceding paragraph , if the obligor is insolvent , the successful bidder may demand total or partial reimbursement of the proceeds against the obligees who received the distribution of the proceeds .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
In the cases set forth in the preceding two paragraphs , if obligors knew of the absence of the object or right and did not disclose the same , or if obligors knew of the absence but demanded an auction , the successful bidder may demand compensation for damages against those persons .
