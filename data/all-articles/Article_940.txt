Administration by Person who has Renounced Inheritance
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A person who has renounced inheritance shall continue the administration of inherited property with the same care he/she would exercise over his/her own property until the person who has become an heir by that renunciation has commenced administration of the inherited property .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
Article 645 , Article 646 , paragraphs -LRB- 1 -RRB- and -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 650 , paragraphs -LRB- 2 -RRB- and paragraph -LRB- 3 -RRB- of Article 918 shall apply mutatis mutandis to the case referred to in the preceding paragraph .
