Partial Assignments of Revolving Mortgages
Before the principal is fixed , a revolving mortgagee may , with the approval of the revolving mortgagor , effect a partial assignment of the Revolving Mortgage -LRB- hereinafter in this Section referring to assignments of Revolving Mortgages that the assignor effects without dividing the Revolving Mortgage in order to co-own the same with the assignee -RRB- .
