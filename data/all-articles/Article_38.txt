Change of Articles of Incorporation
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The articles of incorporation may be changed only if the consent of three-quarters or more of all members is obtained ; provided , however , that , this shall not apply to the cases where it is otherwise provided in the articles of incorporation .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
No change of the articles of incorporation shall take effect unless and until it is approved by the competent government agency .
