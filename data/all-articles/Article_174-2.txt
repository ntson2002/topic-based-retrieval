Extinctive Prescription of Right Established in Judgment of Court
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The period of prescription of any right established in a unappealable judgment shall be ten years even if any period of prescription shorter than ten years is provided .
The same shall apply to any right which is established in a settlement in a court proceeding or conciliation , or any other action which has the effect equivalent to that of the unappealable judgment .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provision of the preceding paragraph shall not apply to any claim which is not yet due and payable yet at the time when the judgment becomes unappealable .
