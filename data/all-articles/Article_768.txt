Distribution of Property
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
One party to a divorce by agreement may claim a distribution of property from the other party .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If the parties do not , or can not , settle on agreement with regard to the distribution of property pursuant to the provision of the preceding paragraph , either party may make a claim to the family court for a disposition in lieu of agreement ; provided that this claim for distribution of property shall be extinguished at the expiration of two years from the day of divorce .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
In the case referred to in the preceding paragraph , the family court shall determine whether to make a distribution , and the amount and method of that distribution , taking into account the amount of property obtained through the cooperation of both parties and all other circumstances .
