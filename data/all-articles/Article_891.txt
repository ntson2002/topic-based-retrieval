Causes of Disqualification of Heir
The following persons may not become an heir : -LRB- i -RRB- a person who has received punishment for intentionally causing , or attempting to cause , the death of a decedent or a person of equal or prior rank in relation to inheritance ; -LRB- ii -RRB- a person who is aware that the decedent was killed by someone but made no accusation or complaint about this ; provided that this shall not apply if that person can not discern right from wrong , or if the killer was that person 's spouse or lineal relative ; -LRB- iii -RRB- a person who prevented a decedent from making , revoking , rescinding , or changing a will relating to inheritance through fraud or duress ; -LRB- iv -RRB- a person who forced a decedent to make , revoke , rescind , or change a will relating to inheritance through fraud or duress ; or -LRB- v -RRB- a person who has forged , altered , destroyed , or concealed a decedent 's will relating to inheritance .
