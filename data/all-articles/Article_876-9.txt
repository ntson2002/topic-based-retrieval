Order Granting Power of Representation to Assistant
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
At the request of a person prescribed by the main clause of paragraph -LRB- 1 -RRB- of Article 15 , an assistant , or a supervisor of an assistant , the family court may make an order that grants power of representation to the assistant , concerning specified juristic acts for the person under assistance .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- and paragraph -LRB- 3 -RRB- of Article 876-4 shall apply mutatis mutandis to the order referred to in the preceding paragraph .
