Liability of Joint Tortfeasors
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If more than one person has inflicted damages on others by their joint tortious acts , each of them shall be jointly and severally liable to compensate for those damages .
The same shall apply if it can not be ascertained which of the joint tortfeasors inflicted the damages .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of the preceding paragraph shall apply to any person who incited or was an accessory to the perpetrator , by deeming him/her to be one of the joint tortfeasors .
