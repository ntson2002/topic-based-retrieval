Disposal Partners' Shares and Division of Partnership Property
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If a partner disposes of his/her share with respect to the partnership property , the disposal may not be asserted against the partnership and third parties who had dealings with the partnership .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
A partner may not seek the division of the partnership property before the same is liquidated .
