Presumption of Renewal of Leases
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In cases where a lessee continues to make use or take the profits of the Thing after the expiration of the term of the lease , if a lessor who knows of the same raises no objection , it shall be presumed that a further lease is entered into under conditions identical to those of the previous lease .
In such cases , each party may request to terminate in accordance with the provisions of Article 617 .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If one of the parties has provided security for the previous lease , the security shall be extinguished upon expiration of the term ; provided , however , that this shall not apply to a security deposit .
