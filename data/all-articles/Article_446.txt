Responsibility of Guarantor
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A guarantor shall have the responsibility to perform the obligation of the principal obligor when the latter fails to perform such obligation .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
No contract of guarantee shall be effective unless it is made in writing .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If a contract of guarantee is concluded by electromagnetic record -LRB- meaning a record produced by electronic means , magnetic means , or any other means unrecognizable by natural sensory functions that is for computer data-processing use -RRB- which records the contents thereof , the contract of guarantee is deemed to be made in writing , and the provision of the preceding paragraph shall apply .
