Money to be refunded by a guardian to a ward and money to be refunded by a ward to a guardian shall bear interest from the time the account of guardianship is settled .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If a guardian has expended a ward 's money for his/her own benefit , such money shall bear interest from the time of the expenditure .
In this case , further damages are incurred by the ward , the guardian shall be liable for such damages .
