Guardian's Duty to Report Claims or Obligations in Relation to Ward
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In the case where a guardian has any claim or bears any obligation in relation to a ward , he/she shall report this to the supervisor , if one exists , before undertaking an investigation of property .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If a guardian knows of a claim against a ward and does not report it , this claim is lost .
