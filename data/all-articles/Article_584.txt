Sale of Co-ownership Interest with Special Agreements on Redemption
If one of the co-owners of immovable property sells his/her equity interest with special agreements on its redemption and the immovable property is then divided or subjected to auction , the seller may redeem the portion or purchase money that the buyer receives or is to receive ; provided , however , that any division or auction effected without notice to the seller may not be asserted against the seller .
