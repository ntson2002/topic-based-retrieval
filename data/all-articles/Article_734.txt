Prohibition of Marriage between Close Relatives
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Neither lineal relatives by blood nor collateral relatives by blood within the third degree of kinship may marry ; provided that this shall not apply between an adopted child and his/her collateral relatives by blood through adoption .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The preceding paragraph shall also apply after the termination of a family relationship pursuant to the provision of Article 817-9 .
