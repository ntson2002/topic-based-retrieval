Filing for Settlement and Conciliation
The filing for settlement or the filing for conciliation under the Civil Conciliation Act -LRB- Act No. 222 of 1951 -RRB- or Family Affairs Adjudication Act -LRB- Act No. 152 of 1947 -RRB- shall not have the effect of interruption of the prescription in cases where , when the counterparty fails to appear in the court or when the settlement or conciliation is not satisfactorily concluded , the action is not brought within one month .
