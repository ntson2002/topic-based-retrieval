Statutory Liens over Movables
A person who has a claim that arose from the causes listed below shall have a statutory lien over certain movables of the obligor : -LRB- i -RRB- A lease of immovable property ; -LRB- ii -RRB- A lodging at a hotel or inn ; -LRB- iii -RRB- The transportation of passengers or luggage ; -LRB- iv -RRB- The preservation of movables ; -LRB- v -RRB- The sale of movables ; -LRB- vi -RRB- The supply of seed or fertilizer -LRB- hereinafter including eggs of silkworms or mulberry leaves used to feed silkworms -RRB- ; -LRB- vii -RRB- Agricultural labor ; or -LRB- viii -RRB- Industrial labor .
