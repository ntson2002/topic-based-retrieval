Supporter under Duty
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Lineal relative by blood and siblings have a duty to support each other .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If special circumstances exist , the family court may also impose a duty of support between relatives within the third degree , in addition to the case prescribed in the preceding paragraph .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If an alteration in circumstances arises after an order pursuant to the provision of the preceding paragraph , the family court may revoke that order .
