Nullity of Adoption
Adoption shall be void only on the following grounds : -LRB- i -RRB- if there is no agreement to the adoption between the parties , as a result of mistaken identity or otherwise ; or -LRB- ii -RRB- if the parties do not give notification of adoption ; provided that the effect of adoption shall not be prevented merely because notification was not in the formality prescribed in paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 739 applied mutatis mutandis to Article 799 .
