Causes of Disqualification of Guardian
Any person who falls under any of the following items may not become a guardian : -LRB- i -RRB- a minor ; -LRB- ii -RRB- a legal representative , curator , or assistant who has been replaced by the family court ; -LRB- iii -RRB- a bankrupt ; -LRB- iv -RRB- a person who has brought or is bringing an action against the ward , or a spouse or lineal blood relative by blood of such person ; or -LRB- v -RRB- a person whose whereabouts are unknown .
