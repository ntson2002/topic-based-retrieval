Making of Special Adoption
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The family court may , at the request of a person to be an adoptive parent , make a ruling establishing an adoption which extinguishes the legal relationship between a child and his/her natural relatives -LRB- referred to in this subsection as ` special adoption ' -RRB- .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The permission referred to in Article 794 and Article 798 is not required for the application referred to in the provision of the preceding paragraph .
