Dissolution of Special Adoption
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
The family court may , at the request of the adopted child , his/her natural parents or a public prosecutor , make a ruling dissolving the adoptive relation , if both of the following items are satisfied and the family court finds it especially necessary for the interests of the adopted child : -LRB- i -RRB- the adoptive parents have abused , or abandoned in bad faith , the adopted child or there is any other ground of extreme harm to the interests of the adopted child ; -LRB- ii -RRB- the natural parent -LRB- s -RRB- are capable of providing reasonable care for the child .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
Dissolution of special adoption shall only be made pursuant to the provision of the preceding paragraph .
