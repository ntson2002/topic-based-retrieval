Residence
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If the domicile of a person is unknown , his/her residence is deemed to be his/her domicile .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If a person does not have the domicile in Japan , his/her residence is deemed to be his/her domicile , whether he/she is a Japanese or a foreign national ; provided , however , that , this shall not apply where the law of domicile controls in accordance with the applicable provision of laws which provide the governing law .
