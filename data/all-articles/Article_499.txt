Voluntary Subrogation
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A person who has performed the obligation for the benefit of an obligor may be subrogated to the claim of the obligee by acquiring the acknowledgment of the obligee upon such performance .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provision of Article 467 shall apply mutatis mutandis to the case set forth in the preceding paragraph .
