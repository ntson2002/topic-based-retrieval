Permission for Occupation
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A child may not have an occupation without the permission of a person who exercises parental authority .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
A person who exercises parental authority may revoke or limit the permission referred to in the preceding paragraph in the case referred to in paragraph -LRB- 2 -RRB- of Article 6 .
