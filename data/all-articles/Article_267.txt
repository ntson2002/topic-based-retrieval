Mutatis Mutandis Application of Provisions regarding Neighboring Relationships
The provisions of Subsection 2 , Section 1 of the preceding Chapter -LRB- Neighboring Relationships -RRB- shall apply mutatis mutandis between superficiaries or between a superficiary and a landowner ; provided , however , that the mutatis mutandis application of the provisions of Article 229 to holders of superficies shall be limited to cases where structures on the boundary line are installed after the creation of the superficies .
