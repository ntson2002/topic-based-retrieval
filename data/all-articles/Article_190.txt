Return of Fruits by Possessors in bad faith
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A possessor in bad faith shall be obligated to return fruits , and reimburse the price of fruits that he/she has already consumed , has damaged due to negligence or has failed to collect .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of the preceding paragraph shall apply mutatis mutandis to persons who possess Thing through violence or duress , or by concealing the same .
