Ownership in Fruit and Payment of Interest on Purchase money
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If any subject matter of a sale that is not delivered yet bears any fruit , the fruit shall vest in the seller .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The buyer shall assume an obligation to pay the interest on the purchase money from the day of delivery ; provided , however , that , if a due date is provided for the payment of the purchase money , it shall not be necessary to pay the interest until that due date arrives .
