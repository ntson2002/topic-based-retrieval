Performance of Obligations of Others
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In cases where a person who is not an obligor has performed an obligation by mistake , if the obligee has , in good faith , allowed the instrument to be lost , damaged the instrument , waived the security or lost the claim by prescription , the person who performed the obligation may not demand the return of the performance .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of the preceding paragraph shall not preclude the person who performed an obligation from exercising his/her right of subrogation against the obligor .
