Assumption of Rights upon Reversion to Previous Surname by Divorce
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If a husband or wife who has taken a new surname by marriage divorces by agreement after inheriting the rights contained in paragraph -LRB- 1 -RRB- of Article 897 , the matter of who will be the successor of those rights shall be determined by agreement of the parties and any other interested persons .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If the agreement of the preceding paragraph is not , or can not be , made , the family court shall determine who will be the successor of the rights in that paragraph .
