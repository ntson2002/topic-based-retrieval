Payment from Assets other than Mortgaged Immovable Properties
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A mortgagee may receive payment from assets other than the Mortgaged Immovable Properties , limited to the extent of the portion of his/her claim not paid from the proceeds of that Mortgaged Immovable Properties .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of the preceding paragraph shall not apply to cases where the proceeds of other assets are to be distributed prior to the proceeds of the Mortgaged Immovable Properties .
In such cases , each other obligee may demand that the amount to be distributed to the mortgagee be deposited in order to have the mortgagee receive payment under the provisions of that paragraph .
