Statutory Ratification
If , after the time when it has become possible to ratify an act pursuant to the provisions of the preceding Article , any of the following events occurs with respect to an act which is otherwise rescindable , it is deemed that ratification has been made , unless any objection is reserved : -LRB- i -RRB- performance of such act , in whole or in part ; -LRB- ii -RRB- demand for the performance of such act ; -LRB- iii -RRB- novation of such act ; -LRB- iv -RRB- provision of security ; -LRB- v -RRB- assignment , in whole or in part , of any right acquired as a result of such rescindable act ; or -LRB- vi -RRB- compulsory execution of such act .
