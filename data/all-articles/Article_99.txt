Requirements and Effect of Act of Agent
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
A manifestation of intention made by an agent representing that the same is made on behalf of the principal within the scope of the agent 's authority binds the principal .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provision of the preceding paragraph shall apply mutatis mutandis to any manifestation of intention made by a third party to an agent .
