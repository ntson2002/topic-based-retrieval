Requirements for Guarantor
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Where an obligor has the obligation to furnish a guarantor , such guarantor must : -LRB- i -RRB- be a person with capacity to act ; and -LRB- ii -RRB- have sufficient financial resources to pay the obligation .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If the guarantor ceases to meet the requirement set forth in item -LRB- ii -RRB- of the preceding paragraph , the obligee may demand that some other person meeting the requirements listed in any item of such paragraph be substituted for such guarantor .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
The provisions of the preceding two paragraphs shall not apply in the case the obligee has designated the guarantor .
