Exercise of Authority where Multiple Guardians of a Minor Exist
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
If there are multiple guardians of a minor , they shall exercise their authority jointly .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
If there are multiple guardians of a minor , the family court may determine ex officio that some of the guardians should only exercise their authority regarding property .
-LRB- 3 -RRB- 
If there are multiple guardians of a minor , the family court may determine ex officio that each guardian should exercise his/her authority individually or that the multiple guardians should exercise their authority according to a division of labor , with regard to the authority regarding property .
-LRB- 4 -RRB- 
The family court may rescind ex officio a determination made pursuant to the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs .
-LRB- 5 -RRB- 
If there are multiple guardians of a minor , it is sufficient that a manifestation of intention by a third party be made to one guardian .
