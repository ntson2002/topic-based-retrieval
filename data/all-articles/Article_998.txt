Warranty Liability of Person Having Obligation for to Testamentary Gift for Unspecified Things
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
In the case where a testamentary gift has as its object unspecified Things but a third party as a rightful claimant retakes them from a testamentary donee , a person having an obligation in relation to testamentary gift shall be liable under the same warranty with respect to those unspecified Things , just as a seller .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
In the case where a testamentary gift has as its object unspecified Things , if those goods are defective , a person having an obligation in relation to testamentary gift shall exchange them for Things that are not defective .
