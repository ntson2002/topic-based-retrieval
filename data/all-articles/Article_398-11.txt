Disposition of Revolving Mortgages
-LRB- 1 -RRB- 
Before the principal is fixed , a revolving mortgagee can not dispose of a Revolving Mortgage under the provisions of Paragraph 1 of Article 376 ; provided , however , that he/she shall not be precluded from applying that Revolving Mortgage to secure other claims .
-LRB- 2 -RRB- 
The provisions of Paragraph 2 of Article 377 shall not apply to payments made before the principal is fixed in the cases provided for in the proviso to the preceding paragraph .
